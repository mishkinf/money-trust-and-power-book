## Overall Assessment

The book is already quite accessible for an economics/history work. The narrative approach, vivid examples (Maureen McKenzie, Edward Backwell, Thomas Fox), and clear explanations make complex concepts understandable. Main improvements needed are:

1. **Define technical terms on first use** (especially monetary jargon)
2. **Break up dense institutional passages** with more white space and subheadings
3. **Add brief clarifying phrases** for assumed knowledge
4. **Strengthen transitions** between major conceptual shifts

The book's strength is showing rather than telling - maintaining this while adding just-in-time definitions will maximize accessibility without dumbing down the content.
