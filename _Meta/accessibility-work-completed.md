## Methodology

### Approach:
1. **Word-for-word reading** of entire book
2. **Systematic identification** of undefined terms
3. **Priority ranking** based on:
   - Frequency of use
   - Criticality to understanding
   - Likelihood of reader unfamiliarity
4. **Inline definitions** added at first use
5. **Glossary expansion** for comprehensive reference

### Definition Style:
- **Concise** - typically one sentence
- **Contextual** - relates to book's themes
- **Natural** - flows with narrative
- **Accurate** - historically and economically precise
- **Accessible** - avoids circular jargon
