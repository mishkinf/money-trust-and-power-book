- Example: "If you're thinking 'but surely printing unlimited money causes inflation,' you're asking exactly the right question. The answer is more nuanced..."

**NO new research needed:** This synthesizes concepts already in book (WWII from Ch 10, Weimar from current Ch 13)

## MAINTAINING BOOK'S ESTABLISHED QUALITIES

### Voice & Tone Consistency

**Study these exemplar sections before writing:**
- Chapter 7, lines 145-178 (Samuel Gigney): Economic tragedy through specific details
- Chapter 10, lines 89-142 (Harry Todd): Family hardship without sentimentality  
- Chapter 8, lines 89-115 (Attwood's petition): Individual embedded in systemic analysis
- Chapter 13, lines 98-142 (McKenzie): Contemporary crisis, compassionate but analytical

**Key stylistic elements to maintain:**

1. **Specific details create impact:**
   - Not "workers suffered" but "seventeen months without wages, April 1926-November 1926"
   - Not "bank failed" but "processed on February 25, 1824 together in what must have been a grim assembly line"
   - Not "lost everything" but "401(k) worth $340,000 in August became worth $12,000 by October"

2. **Date precision:**
   - Every major event gets exact date when available
   - Creates verisimilitude and historical weight
   - Example: "September 15, 2008, Monday morning" not "when Lehman collapsed"

3. **Transitional architecture:**
   - Each section ends with forward bridge: "But understanding why it worked requires examining..."
   - Personal stories flow into systemic analysis naturally
   - Example: "[Individual]'s experience was not isolated. The mechanism that destroyed [him/her] was..."

4. **Analytical restraint:**
   - Present evidence, let readers draw conclusions
   - Avoid "this clearly shows" or "obviously proves"
   - Trust the data: "In the three years following [policy], [outcome occurred]. [Evidence 1]. [Evidence 2]. [Evidence 3]."

5. **Contemporary parallels:**
   - Each chapter opens with modern analogy
   - But avoid forced connections—only draw parallels when genuinely illuminating
   - Chapter 13's opening (COVID stimulus debate) works because it's THE test case
