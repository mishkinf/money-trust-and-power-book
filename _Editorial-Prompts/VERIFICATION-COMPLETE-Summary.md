## Research Finding

**Original Claim:** "Officials expected the suspension to last six weeks"

**Research Result:** **INCORRECT** - No primary source evidence supports "six weeks"

**Accurate Information:**

The "six weeks" claim likely stems from:
1. Pitt's political rhetoric: "a few weeks" (not "six weeks")
2. Rounding of 7-8 week statutory period
3. Confusion with 1803 amendment (6 weeks after parliamentary session)
