## Objective

Restructure the manuscript from 9 chapters to 11 chapters organized into 4 thematic parts. This addresses the pacing problem where early chapters are well-paced but middle chapters become dense and the modern era feels rushed.

### Step 3: Chapter-by-Chapter Migration

For each chapter in the new structure:

1. **Copy source material** to new location
2. **Add chapter header** with:
   - Part number and title
   - Chapter number and title
   - Brief (1-2 sentence) chapter premise
3. **Revise opening paragraph** to connect to previous chapter
4. **Revise closing paragraph** to transition to next chapter
5. **Add "Why This Matters Now"** section (2-3 paragraphs) before chapter summary

### Step 4: Create Bridging Material

Where chapters split or merge, write NEW transitional paragraphs (200-300 words) that:
- Recap what reader just learned
- Preview what's coming next
- Explain why this transition matters for understanding the argument

### Step 5: Update All Cross-References

Search for all instances of "Chapter X" references and update to match new numbering.
